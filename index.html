Online Dating Sites 101

How do they work?

Online Dating is a $2B industry and growing

There are 3 primary 'types' of services:

Subscription based (Match.com)
Ad-driven (OkCupid.com) Group driven (Grouper.com)

'Algorithm' based on fulfilling business goal

Subscription sites = conversion to sale


Ad-driven sites = keep users engaged


Group sites = share us with your friends

Dating algorithms follow a basic logic:

Relative attractiveness (CTR)

Site / Mail Engagement

Age:Gender Match

Geolocation Match

Local Min/Max for Outreach

So how do you make the most of it?

Upload recent pictures of yourself!

Avoid pictures with the opposite sex

Have an idea of what you're looking for

confidence is attractive
people with general, indirect essays get less engagement

Check your ego at the door

You will get rejected!

Understand your dependencies

Dating and online dating can be a full time job
Don't over extend yourself!
